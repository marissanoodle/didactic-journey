Animal Rescue Resources

Thank you for visiting my page! I have put together some information about houseless animals in our community and some ways that we can help make a difference.

Did you know?

  • A recent statistic shows that 1 out of 10 dogs born succesfully obtain a home.
  • 7.6 Million companion animals end up in a shelter nationwide every year.
  • Each year 2.7 million cats and dogs are euthanized due to overcrowding.

How can I help?

  • You can sign up to foster pets at your local shelter!
  • You can donate money, supplies, and your time to a rescue group or shelter of your choice.
  • Advocate for policies that protect animals, such as trap-neuter-return programs.

Animal shelters and rescues rely on monetary donations to save lives and care for homeless animals. Donations help in the cost of running the shelter, including animal food, staff training, upgrades to buildings, veterinary care, community outreach, and animal enrichment program

To find your local rescues and shelters, visit Official Petfinder website
